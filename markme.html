1. Purpose of the App

MarkMe is designed to streamline student attendance using face recognition technology. Additionally, it provides academic assistance through an AI-powered model and allows students to track their attendance and performance.

2. User Eligibility
  • You must be a registered student of the institution.
  • Your facial data (embedding) must be enrolled in the system by authorized personnel.
  • Access to the app is restricted to authorized users only.
3. Attendance Marking Process
  • Students upload their images to generate facial embeddings.
  • Attendance is marked automatically when your face is recognized at the classroom entrance.
  • In case of technical issues, manual attendance may be taken by faculty.
4. Academic Assistance
  • The app includes an AI-powered model to answer questions related to the academic calendar, course curriculum, and other academic topics.
  • Responses are generated based on institutional data and may not always be accurate. Verify critical information with faculty.
5. Data Collection and Privacy
  • The app collects and stores facial embeddings and images for attendance purposes.
  • All data is processed securely and used solely for academic purposes.
  • The app complies with institutional data protection policies and applicable privacy laws.
  • Attendance records and other personal data are accessible only to authorized personnel.
6. Student Responsibilities
  • Ensure your face is visible and unobstructed during attendance capture.
  • Do not attempt to bypass or misuse the system.
  • Report any technical issues or errors to the faculty or IT team.
  • Use the AI-powered model responsibly and avoid asking irrelevant or inappropriate questions.
7. Limitations

The system may not function as intended under the following conditions:

  • Poor lighting or obstructed facial visibility.
  • Technical issues with the device or network.
  • Inaccurate or incomplete data provided by the user.
8. Misuse and Disciplinary Action

  • Such actions may lead to disciplinary measures as per institutional policy.
  • Attempting to extract or misuse data from the system is a violation of these terms.
